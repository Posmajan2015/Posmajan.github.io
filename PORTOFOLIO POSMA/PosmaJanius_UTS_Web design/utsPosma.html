Classically trained in Tokyo, world renowned chef Nobu Matsuhisa has taken traditional Japanese cuisine and infused bold South American and Western flavors to create a style that continues to captivate audiences worldwide. The David Rockwell designed decor, featuring a hand-layed river rock wall, abounds with those who want to be seen, while more discrete sushi lovers apt for a little privacy behind fronds of natural bamboo. Like its award winning namesake, Nobu Las Vegas continues to enchant the palates of fans the world over.
